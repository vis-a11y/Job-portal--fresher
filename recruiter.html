📖 How the Hiring Process Works

A transparent, 7-step skills-first recruitment process.

1

Job Posting

Recruiter creates a detailed job post with title, required skills, description, and eligibility criteria targeting freshers.

2

Application Received

Freshers view job listings, submit profiles, skills, and portfolio projects. Applications arrive in the pipeline.

3

Candidate Screening

Recruiter evaluates each candidate based on skills, projects, education, and the unique Fresher Potential Score.

4

Smart AI Matching

System computes Match % and highlights Skill Gaps automatically, helping recruiters shortlist faster and smarter.

⭐ Unique Feature
5

Shortlisting

Recruiter shortlists qualified candidates and rejects unqualified ones. Candidates are notified of status changes.

6

Interview

Recruiter schedules interview rounds (Technical + HR), sends notifications to candidates, and evaluates communication skills.

7

🎉 Final Hiring

Recruiter selects the best candidate, marks status as "Selected / Hired", and earns a Fresher Employer Badge.
